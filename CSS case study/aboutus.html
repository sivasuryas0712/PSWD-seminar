About Us

Auto Wake is an innovative travel assistance project designed to help users reach their destination with maximum safety and convenience. Our system automatically alerts travellers when they approach their destination, making journeys safer—especially for elderly people, night travellers, and tourists.

We make use of cutting-edge technology such as Geo-Fencing, Google Maps API and location tracking features to deliver accurate and reliable alerts. Our mission is to simplify travel and ensure a stress-free experience for everyone.

Our team constantly works on improving accuracy, speed, and integrating more smart features to enhance user experience. Your safety and comfort are our priority.
